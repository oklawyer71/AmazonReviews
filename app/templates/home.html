Project Overview

We are currently enrolled in a Certificate of Machine Learning program at York University. We are doing this project as part of our second course: CSML1010 Applied Machine Learning and Lifecycle.

Problem Statement

We would like to assess the Amazon Reviews to identify top terms customers are using in their reviews to be able to a) have a better sense of what is most important to customers, and b) if there are any insights to be deduced from the product reviews.
